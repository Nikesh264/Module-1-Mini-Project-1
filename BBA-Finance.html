BBA - Finanace is a three-year undergraduate programme that prepares students for a professional banking qualification and provides a solid foundation in the subject of banking and finance. During this programme, students learn core subjects such as Financial Accounting, Business Economics, Business Mathematics, Principles of Management, and so on, as well as some specialised courses such as Business Taxation, Indian Banking System, Security Analysis, and Investment Management in the field of Banking and Finance. The BBA in Finance programme is designed to provide students with not only fundamental management skills but also specialised knowledge of finance sector operations. The programme also allows students to pursue a postgraduate degree in business administration or specialised industries such as insurance, financial markets, and banking.

This course is ideal for people who are interested in finance and accounting and want to work in the banking, financial services, or insurance industries. Candidates with a basic comprehension of mathematics and an interest in business studies, as well as a sharp and logical mind, are a natural fit for this programme.
