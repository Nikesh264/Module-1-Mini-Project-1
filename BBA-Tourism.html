BBA - Tourism & Event Management is a 3 year specialisation programme that focuses on the application of management skills in the development, planning, marketing, and organization of events. Branding, logistic support, and behavioral science are examples of fundamental programmes. Students learn how to set budgets and buy materials. Many of the classes may cover project management principles. Curriculum topics such as event innovation, business strategy, and efficient messaging are frequently covered. Specialized classes may focus on various event-related industries, such as travel and tourism management, hotel hospitality, and planning and development.

A BBA in Event Management & Tourism can lead to jobs in event planning firms, convention centres, and hotel chains. Tourism is a growing industry with a high demand for skilled professionals in a variety of positions such as tourist guide, product design and development director, and logistics specialist. In the sports and event industries, human resource managers and financial controllers are frequently required. Meeting and event planners with a background in hospitality may find rewarding careers in city or destination marketing.
